Revision vs Editing
}Editing:
}Correcting for grammatical mistakes
}Revision:
}Correcting for clarity and quality of writing
}There may be nothing “wrong”
}might not be the best choice or time
}Ask yourself: “Am I accomplishing what I want to accomplish here? Is this as good I can make it?”
 (Hint: The answer is nearly always “no”)
}Nothing is ever done, it’s just due.
