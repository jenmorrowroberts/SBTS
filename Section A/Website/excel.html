Sample Spreadsheet

The spreadsheet below was made using Excel as a way for me to keep track of progress towards my SMARTR goal for the school year. My collaborative 10th grade team decided to use a common writing rubric, the SAT II writing assessment, which uses a 6-point holistic scale to rate student writing. I set the goal that at least 75% of my students will improve by at least 1 point on the scale by the end of the school year. The excel document allows me to see student progress and share this data with my team and administrator.
